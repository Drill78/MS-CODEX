High Grade Build Divers (HGBD) (ハイグレードビルドダイバーズ, Hai Gurēdo Birudo Daibāzu?) is a line of Gunpla kits of the Mobile Suits from the Gundam Build Divers series. It is split into two lines: the standard HG 1/144 model kits and High Grade Build Custom, which consists of accessories to enhance other Gunpla kits. The HGBD line also includes the sub-line SD Build Divers (SDBD) and Figure-rise Standard BUILD DIVERS (FRSBD).

Following the reorganization of Bandai Namco Group, box covers of reissue and future release of kits from September 2018 onwards feature a blue Bandai logo instead of the original red logo, while releases and reissues from mid-2022 onwards feature the Bandai Namco logo on the upper left corner.

Notes & Trivia[]

  • High Grade Build Divers is the first High Grade product line belonging to a specific Gundam series, where the first model released in the line is not the 'main' suit of that series.
  • Due to some of the Gunpla using runners from their base models, it is entirely possible to remake the base model. This includes:
    • HGBD GM III Beam Master -> HGUC GM III
    • HGBD Gabaldy Rebake -> HGUC Gabaldy Beta
    • HGBD NPD Leo -> HGAC Leo
  • HGBD-022 includes enough leftover parts to build another Gundam if modelers have additional spare parts in their collection.
  • Despite having extra upper body parts, FRSBD-028 lacks several vital joint parts to build a complete bust to display alongside the standard figure.
