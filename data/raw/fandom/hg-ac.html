High Grade After Colony (HGAC) is a line of 1/144 scale Gunpla model kits of the Mobile Suits from the After Colony timeline of the Gundam meta series. First produced in 2013, the line is a spin-off of the High Grade Universal Century line, with it's releases being numbered as such.

To prepare for the 40th anniversary of Gunpla in 2020, the "Gunpla Evolution Project" began in 2017. Showcasing improvements and innovations in specific areas, this project involved Gunpla from multiple lines, with one of them being a kit of the OZ-06MS Leo.

Following the reorganization of Bandai Namco Group, box covers of reissue and future release of kits from September 2018 onwards feature a blue Bandai logo instead of the original red logo, while releases and reissues from mid-2022 onwards further feature the Bandai Namco logo on the upper right corner.

Trivia[]

  • Due to the small size of Mobile Suits from the After Colony timeline, HGAC kits are actually modeled in 1/132 scale.
