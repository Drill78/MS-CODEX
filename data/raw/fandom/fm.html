Full Mechanics (FM) is a line of Gunpla established in 2021, designed to feature certain mobile suits in 1/100 scale but separate from the Master Grade (MG) line. In contrast with the Reborn-One Hundred (RE/100) line, this line features mobile suits from timelines outside of Universal Century only. Aside from the Gundam franchise, the Full Mechanics line has hosted other mecha anime series such as Amaim Warrior at the Borderline.

This line should not be confused with the 1/100 IRON-BLOODED ORPHANS Full Mechanics line from 2016, which is likely its predecessor.

This article covers only the Gundam releases.
