Real Grade (RG) (リアルグレード, Riarugurēdo?) is a line of Gunpla kits that was introduced by Bandai in 2010 as part of the Gundam 30th Anniversary commemoration project. These consist of Mobile Suits from both the Universal Century and the Alternate Centuries. The RG line combines the best aspects of the other three Gunpla lines: The small size and affordable prices of the High Grade, the advanced inner frame technology of the Master Grade and the massive parts count and details of the Perfect Grade. The tagline for the RG line is "Excitement Embodied".[1] Also, the RG line reflects what a mobile suit would look like if it were built in the present day.

The RG line was previously known for the Advanced MS Joint, using a dual-molding technique with both ABS and PP plastics. Initially, the inner frame of RG suits were placed on a single gate for easy construction, along with hands with poseable fingers. However, by 2018, the Advanced MS Joint began to be slowly phased out, as it became apparent that they did not have enough strength to support the kit in an upright position. RG kits began to make less and less use of this gimmick except for more complex parts. Poseable fingers were also replaced by fixed hands for a similar reason. Starting from August 2024, with the release of the RX-78-2 Gundam Ver.2.0, kits may only feature a Perfect Grade-like inner frame and eliminate Advanced MS Joints completely.

Following the reorganization of Bandai Namco Group, box covers of reissue and future release of kits from September 2018 onward feature blue Bandai logo instead of the original red logo.

Starting in 2020, the Real Grade line expanded beyond the Gundam franchise with mecha from Rebuild of Evangelion and The King of Braves GaoGaiGar. From here onward, the Real Grade logo was modified to exclude "144", even though the Gundam releases are still predominantly 1/144 scale.

Starting in 2022, the Real Grade logo was moved from the top right corner of the box art to above the model's name to make room for the Bandai Namco logo on both reissued kits and new releases.

This article covers only the Gundam releases.

Notes & Trivia[]

  • Currently no Real Grade kits based on Mobile Suits from the After War, Correct Century, Advanced Generation, Regild Century, Post Disaster or Ad Stella timelines have been produced.
  • With the exception of Mobile Suits that have only appeared in manga, currently all Real Grade kits are from productions that have received an English dub.
  • Sinanju and 00 Qan[T] have been the only Real Grade releases within five years of their source material that were not a retooling of an existing Real Grade.
  • RG-30 Full-Armor Unicorn is the largest Real Grade regular release to date.
  • RG-41 Akatsuki is the most expensive Real Grade regular release to date.
  • RG-06 Skygrasper is the smallest Real Grade regular release to date.
  • RG Skygrasper,RX-78-2 2.0 and Wing Zero TV are the only Real grade Regular Releases to not include any ADVANCED MS Joints or MS FRAMEWORK.
