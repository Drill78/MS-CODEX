The High Grade Reconguista in G (HGRG) is a line of 1/144 scale Gunpla model kits of the Mobile Suits from the TV series Gundam Reconguista in G and the compilation film series Reconguista in G the Movie of the Gundam meta series.

Following the reorganization of Bandai Namco Group, box covers of reissue and future release of kits from September 2018 onwards feature a blue Bandai logo instead of the original red logo, while releases and reissues from mid-2022 onwards further feature the Bandai Namco logo on the upper right/left corner.
