High Grade Gundam Build Metaverse (HGGBM) (ハイグレードガンダムビルドメタバース, Hai Gurēdo Gandamu Birudo Metabāsu?) is a line of Gunpla kits of the Mobile Suits from the Gundam Build Metaverse series. Like its predecessors High Grade Build Fighters and High Grade Build Divers, HGGBM includes multiple sub-lines: Entry Grade Gundam Build Metaverse (EGGBM), SD Gundam Build Metaverse (SDGBM) and Figure-rise Standard Gundam Build Metaverse (FRSGBM).
